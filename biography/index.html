Biography

Shaobing Xu is an assistant research scientist working with Prof. Huei Peng at the Department of Mechanical Engineering (ME) and Mcity, University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. He received his Ph.D. degree in Mechanical Engineering from Tsinghua University, Beijing, China, in 2016. His research focuses on motion control, decision making, and path planning for automated and connected vehicles. He is currently concentrating on developing self-driving technologies and addressing engineering and theoretical challenges in this process.
